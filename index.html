About Us

Trusted by thousands of farmers across Uganda

Country Farm Matugga has been Uganda's trusted source for premium day-old chicks for over 15 years. We specialise in high-quality broiler, layer, and Kuroiler breeds raised under strict biosecurity and veterinary oversight.

Our state-of-the-art hatchery uses certified parent stock and maintains controlled temperature and humidity from day one, giving every chick the best possible start. This translates directly into the 95% survival rate our farmers rely on.

With over 500,000 chicks delivered and 2,000+ satisfied farmers, we remain committed to empowering Ugandan poultry farmers with the healthiest, strongest chicks ‚Äî delivered right to your farm.
